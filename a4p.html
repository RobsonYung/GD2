Swiss Style

The design of this webpage is based on Swiss Style, which has a great influence on the Graphic Design Industry. Many web designs nowadays contain characteristics of Swiss Style such as use of grids, asymmetrical layouts and san serif typefaces, etc. However, I started to have this idea of using Swiss Style design in the fairly late stage of this assignment. Hence, you may not see many Swiss Style elements in my process.
