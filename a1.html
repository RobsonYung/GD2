ROBSON YUNG .-.. --- ...- . ... GRAPHIC DESIGN 2

A1: CONFOUNDING TOOLS

:design a 24” × 36” digital poster about my life during COVID 19. The rule is that it must not be made by existing design software.

Class 1

My idea is to use Spotify, which was one of my most frequently-used apps during Covid, as the main component of my poster. I decided to deliver my message in the most direct way which is with texts. So, I planned to combine some song names to form a sentence that describes my life under the pandemic.

Class 1

I used some random songs to try out the idea of forming a sentence using song names. I have also decided to use Google Slide as my design tool to compose my poster.

Class 2

I thought of a sentence to describe my life during Covid 19: Hi everyone, one fun fact about me is that I don’t even know where my school at. For the next step, I searched for the songs with names that can fit in my sentence and tried different layouts. I have also added some album covers that contain Covid-related themes in some of the compositions.

Feedback: Those album covers are drawing the attention away from the main message of the poster, which is the sentence.

Class 4

I removed the album covers as suggested. Then, I decided to return to basics and make use of the layout of Spotify. I have created multiple layouts that are more simple.

Feedback: Add more songs to increase the length of the sentence

Class 6

I added more song names to the sentence: Hi everyone! I’m in my second year of Graphic Design, one fun fact about me is that I don’t even know where my school at. This allowed me to fill in more space of the poster using the sentence. Lastly, I have made a few variations of the final piece with small differences.
